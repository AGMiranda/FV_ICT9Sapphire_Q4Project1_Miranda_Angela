CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Crisostomo Ibarra
    Personality
  • Crisostomo Ibarra is a compassionate and witty man, who isnt afraid to speak his mind.
  • Although he may look refined and serious to some people, he is very playful around the people he's close to
      • Background
  • Crisostomo Ibarra grew up in San Diego with Maria Clara (CHILDHOOD FRIENDS TO LOVERS!!!!!), but moved to Europe to study.
  • However, he returned home after 7 years of studying when he heard about his father's death
    		•
    Maria Clara
      Personality
  • Maria Clara is a young woman, she is loyal and the model daughter.
  • Just like her fiancee, she isnt afraid to speak her mind, she may be the model daughter, but she can also be rebellious, not afraid to break the gender norms of the time.
      • Background
  • Maria Clara grew up with Crisostomo Ibarra, but instead of joining him in Europe, she was sent to study in the convent of Sta. Clara.
  • The day after Capitan Tiago's party, Ibarra came back home and the two of them reunited.
    		•
    Capitan Tiago
      Personality
  • Kapitan Tiago is a rich Filipino, who values his and his family's image.
  • Although he is very close with the high ranking men in the church, he does not belive in God.
      • Background
  • Kapitan Tiago is the legal father of Maria Clara
  • On the day Ibarra came back, he held a welcome party for him, but when Padre Damaso says he doesn't approve of Maria and Ibarra's relationship, he agrees.
    		•
    Padre Damaso
      Personality
  • Padre Damaso is a corrupt, high ranking priest who values power above all.
  • Even though Padre Damaso was the man who murdered Ibarra's father, he still openly mocks Ibarra.
      • Background
  • When Padre Damaso moved to San Diego, he became close with Capitan Tiago.
  • When he joins Ibarra's welcoming party, he openly shows his disdain for Ibarra. He insults him during the party and while giving a sermon.
